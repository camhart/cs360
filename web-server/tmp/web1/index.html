Old is the New New

Here at oldstylewebdesign we believe that the current fads in web design have gotten out of control.

You don't need java and ajax and php and databases to create and informational and asthetically pleasing website. All this accomplishes is making your site more complicated and more prone to crashing when a surge of traffic comes your way.

How many times have you been linked to a site only to see that there is a wordpress error because it couldn't handle all of the hits? Many would say that you should get a better web server, but that's like saying that in order to make my car go faster that I need a bigger engine. That is one solution.

Another is just to make it more efficient. If all you have on your site is information, then all you need is straight html. This will handle all of your text loading as well as give you many options like putting some jazzy pictures or gifs to make it more pleasing to the eye. All the colors of the rainbow and more are available, why would you want to go all web 2.0 just to get your information out onto the information superhighway?

You don't need a Jaguar just to get to the end of the block, sometimes all you need is a Yugo.
